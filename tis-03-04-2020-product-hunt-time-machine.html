    TID Product Hunt Time Machine

    Marc 3 avril 2020 TID

    To discover new things, a lot of web resources exist. You can use google to have informations on new trends, follow your favourite tech news blog or subscribe to RSS feed via Feedly for example. Few months ago, I discovered Product Hunt, where people and companies can submit their new product and the community vote everyday for the best new one. 

    A lot of products are interesting but sometimes, only the most voted one is really interesting (we can't disrupt everything everyday, right?) That's why exists Product Hunt Time Machine, compiling all the "most voted hunt from every day since Product Hunt records began".  

    With this list, there is no reason to discover new things easily! 

     

    Enjoy!
