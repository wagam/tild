    TID 27-03-2020 NovoResume

    Marc 27 mars 2020 Personal development

    I needed to recreate a resume and I didn't want to do it with MS Word or with Latex as I did before. As a web developer, i was almost sure some web resources exist. After googling for few minutes, I discovered Novo Resume .

    The website is very user friendly and I created my resume in less than an hour with a good result. You can choose differents templates and customize them with differents colors or fonts. Some of them are free, other can be used only with an upgraded account.

    Upgraded account brings more templates and more customization. You still can try all these option to your free account but you will not be able to download your resume. 

    All your changes are automatically saved and you can download your resume in PDF. 

    It is easy! You should try it !
